FINANCE INTERN — LOGIXHEALTH — JUN 2024–SEPT 2024
LogixHealth · Bedford, MA

Finance
Intern.

Healthcare revenue cycle finance — partnering with the FP&A team to bring structure and accuracy to billing, collections, and provider financial reporting.

Role details

FP&A Partnership

Billing & collections analysis

  • Partnered directly with the FP&A team to analyze billing and collections data across LogixHealth's nationwide provider network.
  • Built and maintained financial reports tracking revenue cycle performance metrics — identifying trends in collections rates, denial patterns, and payer mix.
Provider Coordination

Accuracy at scale

  • Coordinated with nationwide providers to reconcile data discrepancies and improve financial reporting accuracy across multiple billing systems.
  • Served as a liaison between internal finance teams and external providers to resolve outstanding billing issues and reporting gaps.
Context

Healthcare finance is different

LogixHealth is a healthcare billing and revenue cycle management company working across emergency medicine, radiology, and other specialties. Finance in this space means navigating payer complexity, claims data at scale, and multi-stakeholder coordination — a materially different operating environment than corporate finance. This internship built the muscle for working with large, messy, real-world datasets under real business consequences.
