Fear-Z Engine

Volatility regime classifier

  • Raw VIX is noisy and absolute. Fear-Z converts it into a z-score relative to a rolling historical window: VIXZ = (VIX_current − VIX_mean) / VIX_std.
  • Maps to three regimes: Episodic (short-term spike, fast decay), Structural (sustained elevation, medium decay), Systemic (crisis-level dislocation, slow decay).
  • The lambda decay parameter controls how long the fear signal persists — fast for episodic noise, slow for systemic crises where fear should weigh longer.
  • Acts as a gating mechanism in the BUY/HOLD/SELL engine, preventing high-EV signals from being acted on in the wrong risk environment.
